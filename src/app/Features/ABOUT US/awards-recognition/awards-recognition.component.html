‘Horn Please’ – Coffee Table Book: An Initiative to Honour Indian Truck Drivers

Mr. Pawan Jain, Founder and Chairman, Safexpress and Ms. Divya Jain, Founder and CEO, Safeducate, co-authored India’s first ever coffee table book, ‘Horn Please’. This book is a unique initiative to make the masses familiar with the life of ‘Truckers’. The book is an attempt to examine the facts and stories at close quarters pertaining to ‘Trucking’ in India and the idea is to access those people who hold the key to devising a fresh set of rules and norms in order to enable these unsung truckers to get their due.
